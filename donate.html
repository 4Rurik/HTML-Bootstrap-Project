Make A Donation

Every dollar you donate goes directly to helping animals in need. The New Hope Animal Shelter does not receive any government grants and relies solely on the community's generosity to keep their doors open. We are a registered charitable organization and provide tax receipts for all monetary donations.

Alternatively, we accept other kinds of donations. See all our options on how to donate below!

Ways to donate:

  • Monetary donation
  • Canned Pet food
  • Medicine (Veterinary approved)
  • Pet Housing: Dog/Cat beds, cages, etc.
  • Pet Toys
